The very famous Shiro

Shiro is a beloved Ethiopian stew made primarily from spiced, powdered chickpeas or broad beans. It's known for its creamy, smooth texture and rich, savory flavor, often prepared with aromatic ingredients like onions, garlic, ginger, and sometimes berbere. Shiro is a staple, especially during fasting periods, and is almost always enjoyed scooped up with pieces of injera.

  6. Season with salt, berbere (optional), and other spices like niter kibbeh or oil.
  7. Serve hot with injera.
